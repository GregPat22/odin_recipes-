Pizza Margherita

Ingredients:

  • Manitoba Flour (200g)
  • Water (300ml)
  • Flour 00 (300g)
  • Fine salt (10g)
  • Fresh brewer's yeast (4g)
    • For the topping:
    • Tomato puree (300g)
    • mozzarella cheese (200g)
    • basil ( to taste )
    • Extra virgin olive oil ( to taste )

Preparations:

    For the Dough:

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Sprinkle the yeast over the water and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt.
  4. Make a well in the center and pour the yeast mixture into the well.
  5. Gradually mix the flour into the liquid until a dough forms.
  6. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10-15 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  7. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1-1.5 hours or until doubled in size.
  8. Preheat the oven to its highest setting (usually around 475-500°F or 245-260°C).

    9. For the Tomato sauce:

  9. Gather the ingredients for the tomato sauce: canned tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, and fresh basil (optional).
  10. Heat a bit of olive oil in a pan and sauté minced garlic until fragrant
  11. Add the canned tomatoes and crush them with a fork or spoon.
  12. Simmer the sauce on low heat for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  13. Season with salt and add fresh basil leaves if desired. Remove from heat.

    14. Assembling and Baking the Pizza:

  14. Once the dough has risen, divide it into two portions for two pizzas.
  15. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and shape.
  16. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  17. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border at the edges.
  18. Add fresh mozzarella cheese evenly on top of the sauce.
  19. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza.
  20. Place a few fresh basil leaves on top.
  21. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and slightly bubbly.
  22. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
